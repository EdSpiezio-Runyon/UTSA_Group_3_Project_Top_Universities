Top US Colleges

In this study we are looking at the top ranked U.S. colleges and looking at the different factors that affect every different student recruit.
The Top 10 are as follows:
1.Princeton: Princeton, NJ, Est: 1746
2.Harvard: Cambridge, MA, Est: 1636
3. (tied) Univ. of Chicago: Chicago, IL, Est: 1890
3. (tied) Yale University: Newhaven, CT, Est: 1701
5. (tied) Columbia: New York, NY, Est: 1754
5. (tied) Stanford: Stanford, CA, Est: 1891
7. M.I.T.: Cambridge, MA, Est: 1861
8. (tied) Duke: Durham, NC Est: 1924
8. (tied) Univ. of Pennsylvania: Philadelphia, PA, Est: 1740
10. Johns Hopkins: Baltimore, MD, Est: 1876

This information was found using data about Top Colleges from two different Kaggle sources that provided a CSV files.
We cleaned and merged the data in SQL.
A Flask API was created from the combined data and is utilized on this website.
Plotly and Charts.js were used to plot the data images seen on this website.
